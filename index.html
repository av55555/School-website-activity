Gateway Public Schools website

Gateway Public School serves Junior Kindergarten to Grade 6 students in the vibrant neighbourhood of Flemingdon Park.

Hi!

Our school population is close to 900 students. Gateway is a diverse community, a representation of the multicultural centre Toronto has become. We celebrate and are dedicated to promoting student success in an inclusive and respectful environment. We hold high expectations for the achievement and the conduct of our students based on the key components of mutual respect, responsibility, safety and commitment to the betterment of self and the broader community.

Every year Gateway organizes a musical which student sing and dress up as a caracter. They practice for 2-3 monthes and then parents are invited to watch.

There is also a DPA dance crew who performs at the end of eavh assembly.


There is also a math club which is called matematica. Students get to learn more and have good practice.

There is a knitting club too, students are privided with yarn and knitting sticks.

Too stay fit the school organizes a track and field program. Students get to do long jumps and races.

There are soccer clubs for girls and boys. Students also get to play against other schools.

There are basketball teams for girls and boys.
